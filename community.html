Community Guidelines

Last Updated: July 2026

Numerology Calculator 360 aims to foster an inclusive, supportive, and spiritually uplifting community. We expect all users to respect these guidelines.

1. Respect and Kindness

Esoteric systems are highly personal and subjective. Treat other seekers with empathy, patience, and kindness. Respect differing interpretations, cultural viewpoints, and opinions.

2. No Misleading Claims

Avoid presenting numerological numbers as definitive scientific facts, medical diagnoses, or financial predictions. Help others understand the self-reflective nature of the calculations.

3. Feedback & Bug Reports

When reporting issues or proposing features, please use clean, non-aggressive, constructive language. Help us maintain a positive and productive environment for all users.
