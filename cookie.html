Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Our website utilizes cookies to ensure a smooth, secure, and personalized user experience.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device by websites. They help the site remember preferences, recognize returning visits, and monitor anonymous traffic trends.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the core technical functions of our website.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use basic, anonymous analytics services to track page views and traffic origins, helping us improve layout usability.

3. Controlling Cookies

You can adjust, disable, or block cookies through your individual browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies might alter the layout or interactivity of some features on our pages.
