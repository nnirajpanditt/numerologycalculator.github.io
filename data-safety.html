Data Safety

Last Updated: July 2026

Google Play demands transparency regarding user data handling. This Data Safety declaration outlines how we protect and manage your data.

Our Core Security Guarantees

  • No Data Shared: We do not share any user data with third-party companies or external organizations, except for anonymous device identifiers utilized by Google AdMob.
  • No Data Collected: The app does not collect personal names, dates of birth, emails, or phone numbers on any server database.
  • Encrypted in Transit/Rest: Since your calculations are saved locally inside a Hive database on Android, your data is fully encrypted at rest using local sandboxing. Any analytics or bug reports you send voluntarily are encrypted in transit via SSL.
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data. Clearing the application storage cache in your Android settings immediately and permanently deletes all calculations, scores, journal entries, and saved profiles.
