Delete Account Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Because we value your privacy and local autonomy, our user account system is completely decentralized.

1. No Account Signup

Numerology Calculator 360 does not require you to create an account, log in with social networks, or submit email credentials to access the app's 20+ calculators. There are no user accounts hosted on our servers.

2. How to Delete Your Data

All profiles, history logs, daily streaks, and customized inputs are saved on your physical device. To permanently delete all data:

  1. Open your Android device's Settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps or Application Manager.
  3. Select Numerology 360.
  4. Tap on Storage.
  5. Select Clear Data and Clear Cache.

This action is irreversible and completely wipes all records from your device.
