Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our calculators

What is the difference between Pythagorean and Chaldean numerology?

The Pythagorean system uses the numbers 1 through 9 to map letters. It is the most common Western framework. The Chaldean system, developed in ancient Babylon, is based on sound vibrations and uses numbers 1 through 8. In the Chaldean system, the number 9 is considered sacred and is excluded from calculations except when it represents the final sum.

Why does the app run fully offline?

We believe your personal numbers, birthdays, and names represent highly private identity signals. By processing all calculations on-device, we guarantee that your data is safe and never leaves your phone.

How is the Lo Shu Grid calculated?

The Lo Shu Grid is generated using your date of birth. The numbers are mapped onto a 3x3 magic square. The presence or absence of numbers indicates active element lines (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water) in your personality profile.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. Numerology Calculator 360 is completely free to download and use. We display banner ads to offset server and maintenance costs, but all calculators remain accessible.
