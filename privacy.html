Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Numerology Calculator 360, your privacy is our absolute priority. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in our mobile application and website.

1. 100% Offline Calculations

All calculations, including dates of birth, full names, local journal entries, and compatibility queries, are processed directly on your physical device. We do not transmit, upload, or store this data on any external servers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal identity information, location coordinates, financial transactions, or communication logs. The app runs fully local. For ad integrations (provided by Google AdMob), anonymous device advertising identifiers may be used to display relevant sponsors in compliance with local privacy frameworks.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app incorporates Google AdMob SDK. Google may collect device-specific analytics, location-based optimization data, and advertising indicators. You can manage personalization preferences directly inside Google Settings on your Android device.

4. Policy Updates

We reserves the right to update this policy as new features roll out. Any changes will be updated on this page with a revised date stamp.
