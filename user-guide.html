User Guide

Master all app features

1. Getting Started

When you open the app, enter your birth date and full birth name to configure your primary profile. The dashboard will instantly generate your main cosmic numbers: Life Path, Birthday Number, and Expression.

2. Managing Profiles

You can add and save profiles for friends, family members, or historical figures. Switch between profiles to inspect their Pythagorean grids or Chinese Lo Shu Magic Squares.

3. Generating Compatibility Reports

Navigate to the Compatibility tab, select two saved profiles, and tap calculate. The screen displays a Venn diagram overlap detailing alignment scores for love, family resonance, and communication.

4. Exporting Reports

To share a chart, tap the Export PDF button at the bottom of any detailed reading screen. The app will generate a clean document that you can save or send.
